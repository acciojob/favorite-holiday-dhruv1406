Gangtok

Gangtok is an incredible stunning hill resort in India, which is situated at an elevation of 1700 meters above the sea level. The charming hill-station leaves the tourists awed with its utter natural charm, snow-covered mountain peaks, stunning valleys and lush green surroundings. The climate of the city gives an unmatched holidaying experience to the guests.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Deer Park
  2. Himalayan Zoological Park
  3. Tsomgo Lake
  4. Pemayangtse Monastery
  5. Nathula Pass

Must Try Foods

  • Thukpa
  • Thenthuk
  • Phagshapa
  • Chhurpi Soup
